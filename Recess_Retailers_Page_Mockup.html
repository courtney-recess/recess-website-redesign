Why retail media networks partner with Recess

A new experiential media capability, beyond the store.

Retail media reaches shoppers at and near the point of purchase. Recess extends your network off-site, engaging your shoppers earlier through real-world experiences in the everyday venues that over-index for them, while still connecting back to retail outcomes.

📍

Expanded Media Inventory

Extend retail media beyond digital and in-store placements with experiential inventory across fitness studios, residential communities, college campuses, and community events, reaching consumers outside the store while still connecting back to retail outcomes.

💰

Access to Incremental Brand Budgets

A large share of the marketing that influences retail sales, including experiential, sponsorships, and brand programs, happens outside retail media. Recess lets your network capture net-new national brand budgets with programs tied directly to retail performance.

⚙️

Minimal Operational Complexity

Recess operates independently of your infrastructure and manages campaigns end to end. No store operations, field teams, or new technology to deploy. It is an external capability that extends your media offering.
